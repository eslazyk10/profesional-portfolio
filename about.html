About

Emily Slazyk




Emily is an entry-level Front-End Developer with experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and React. She has quickly learned to apply these learned skills by creating multiple applications within a short period of time. She enjoys taking on new challenges and won't stop until the challenge is completed the right way.

Emily grew up in Illinois, but has also lived in Austin, TX and most recently, Nashville, TN where she currently still resides with her boyfriend Nick and their dog Gus. She previously held a career in healthcare as a Physical Therapist Assistant and holds multiple certifications and degrees in the field. In her spare time, Emily enjoys travelling to new destinations and exploring the area for fun new experiences and restaurants.
